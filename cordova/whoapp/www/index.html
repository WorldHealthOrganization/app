How it Spreads

COVID-19 also referred to as "Coronavirus" mainly spreads from person-to-person between people who are in close contact with one another (within about 6 feet or two meters) through respiratory droplets produced when an infected person coughs or sneezes

Clean Your Hands

Wash your hands often with soad and water for at least 20 sconds, especially after you have been in a public place, or after blowing your nose, coughing or sneezing.

Avoid touching your eyes, nose, and mouth with unwashed hands

Avoid Close Contact

Avoid close contact with people who are sick.

Maintain distance between yourself and other people if COVID-19 is spreading in you community. This is especially important for people who are at higher risk of getting very sick including the elderly
